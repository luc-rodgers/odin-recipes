French Toast


Description


This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.


Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot.
